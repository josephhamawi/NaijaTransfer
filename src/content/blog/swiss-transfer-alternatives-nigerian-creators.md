## The Challenge of International File Transfers from Nigeria

Several factors contribute to the difficulty of using international file transfer services from Nigeria:

*   **Internet Infrastructure:** While improving, internet speeds in Nigeria can be inconsistent. Upload speeds are particularly affected. Services not built with resilient transfer protocols can fail repeatedly.
*   **Distance and Latency:** Uploading to servers geographically distant from Nigeria introduces latency. This means each packet of data takes longer to travel, slowing down the overall transfer.
*   **Payment Gateways:** Many premium international services require payment in foreign currency, often via credit cards. Nigerian bank cards frequently face international transaction limits or outright declines, making it difficult to access paid tiers that offer more storage or faster speeds.
*   **Compression:** Some services might compress files to save bandwidth, degrading the quality of professional media. Creators need to send files as-is.
