## The Problem with Dollar-Denominated Services

Many global file transfer solutions, including MASV, price their services in US dollars. For businesses and freelancers in Nigeria, this presents several challenges:

*   **Exchange Rate Fluctuations:** The Naira's value against the dollar can change, making budgeting unpredictable. What costs one amount today could cost more tomorrow.
*   **Card Declines:** Nigerian bank cards often face issues when used for international transactions. This isn't a reflection of the agency's creditworthiness but a common gateway restriction. Repeated declines lead to frustration and lost time.
*   **Virtual Card Hassle:** Some users resort to virtual dollar cards. This adds an extra layer of complexity, often involves additional fees, and still doesn't guarantee success.
*   **Unnecessary Markup:** International transactions can sometimes incur hidden fees or markups, increasing the effective cost beyond the advertised price.

When you need to send large files regularly, these recurring issues can significantly disrupt your workflow and impact client relationships. The focus shifts from creative output to troubleshooting payment and transfer problems.
